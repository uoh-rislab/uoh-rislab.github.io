What We Do?


UOH RISLab is an advanced robotics and computational intelligence research laboratory, formed by undergraduate and graduate students and researchers. All members work at the Robotics and Intelligent Systems Laboratory of the Universidad de O'Higgins, with the support of the Institute of Engineering Sciences of the same university.

Robotic Platforms

We work with existing robotic platforms to develop applied research.

UAV

We use state-of-the-art robotic platforms for their application in agriculture.

Educational Robotics

We generate learning opportunities to teach and prepare new young people.

Novel sensors

We work with new and novel sensors, contributing to new fields of research.
